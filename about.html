HELLO! MY NAME IS MEET

I, Meet owner of Meet Borad's Photography since 2010 based in Surat - Gujarat & have covered more than 110 Wedding Photographies till now. My approach is unobtrusive and editorial, with just the right mix of beautiful portraits of you, your family and friends. This style allows me to document the real, unscripted moments that happen at your wedding, capturing true emotions and relationships, and enabling you to really enjoy every moment of your wedding day. The albums you receive after your wedding will allow you and your family to relive those moments, over and over again.
